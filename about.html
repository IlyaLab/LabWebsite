People

The Center for Systems Analysis of the Cancer Regulome consists of a close collaboration between the Institute for Systems Biology and MD Anderson Cancer Center. Together they constitute a Genome Data Analysis Center (GDAC) for The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA).
